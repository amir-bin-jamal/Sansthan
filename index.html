Agriculture is the mainstay of the economy in Uttar Pradesh and sugar industry is the most important and widespread industry of the state. In order to increase the average yield per hectare of sugarcane in the economic development of the sugar industry of U. P., Honorable Mr. Narayan Dutt Tiwari jifirst encouraged the sugarcane farmers of the state not only in India but also in the cultivation of sugarcane. With his inspiration and strong efforts Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Farmers Institute was formed. On February 6, 1975, the institute started the training program for sugarcane farmers. Thereafter, on May 17, 1975, the Institute was registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

PURPOSE OF THE INSTITUTE

  • To train farmers and departmental officers on scientific methods of sugarcane production.
  • To make the training easy and accessible, conducting mobile programs through modern audio-visual equipments.
  • To promote the progressive cultivation of sugarcane by publishing letters, magazines, pamphlets and posters etc.
  • Organizing farmer fairs, exhibitions, seminars and group meetings related to sugarcane development.
  • Organizing annual study tours of farmers/officers in the state, country and abroad to acquaint them with the latest scientific methods and management processes of sugarcane breeding and agriculture.
